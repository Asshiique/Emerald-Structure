2. Information We Collect

We collect information necessary to operate the school management system:

  • Student data: Name, date of birth, gender, blood group, class/section, roll number, admission number, previous school.
  • Parent/Guardian data: Name, phone number, WhatsApp number, email address, home address.
  • Staff data: Name, employee ID, department, class assignment, contact details.
  • Attendance records: Daily present/absent/late status per student.
  • Fee records: Term-wise fee amounts, due dates, and payment status.
  • Homework and notices: Content posted by teachers and administrators.
  • Account credentials: Email address and encrypted password (managed by Firebase Authentication — passwords are never stored in plaintext).

3. How We Use Your Information

  • Provide parents with real-time access to their child's attendance, fees, homework, and school notices.
  • Allow teachers to mark attendance and post homework for their classes.
  • Enable administrators to manage student and staff records.
  • Send automated reminders for overdue fees and daily attendance summaries to staff.
  • Maintain secure, role-based access so only authorized users see relevant data.

4. Data We Do NOT Collect

  • We do not collect location data.
  • We do not serve advertisements.
  • We do not sell or share student or parent data with any third party for commercial purposes.
  • We do not use tracking cookies or behavioral analytics.

5. Third-Party Services

The App uses the following trusted third-party services to function:

  • Google Firebase — Authentication and Firestore real-time database. Firebase Privacy Policy ↗
  • Google Cloud Platform — Backend hosting and data storage. Google Cloud Privacy Notice ↗
  • Expo / React Native — Mobile application framework. Expo Privacy Policy ↗

No data is shared with any other third parties.

6. Data Retention

Student and parent records are retained for the duration of the student's enrollment at Emerald International School and for up to 3 years after leaving, as required for academic record-keeping. You may request deletion of your account data by contacting the school office.

7. Data Security

  • All data is transmitted over HTTPS (TLS encryption).
  • Passwords are managed by Firebase Authentication and are never stored by the school.
  • API endpoints are protected by Firebase JWT token verification.
  • Role-based access control ensures parents can only see their own child's data.
  • Rate limiting is applied on all API routes to prevent unauthorized bulk access.

8. Children's Privacy

This App is operated by a school and is intended for use by parents/guardians on behalf of their children, and by school staff. Student accounts are not created directly — all student data is managed by the school administration and accessed by parents through their verified parent accounts.

9. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access the personal data we hold about you or your child.
  • Request correction of inaccurate data.
  • Request deletion of your account and associated data.
  • Withdraw consent for data processing (note: this may limit app functionality).

To exercise these rights, contact the school office at emeraldinternationalschoolmkd@gmail.com.

10. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. When we do, we will post a notice within the App and update the "Last updated" date above. Continued use of the App after any changes constitutes acceptance of the revised policy.
